Debriefer

An after action report tool

“If you know the enemy and know yourself, you need not fear the result of a hundred battles.
If you know yourself but not the enemy, for every victory gained you will also suffer a defeat.
If you know neither the enemy nor yourself, you will succumb in every battle.”

- Sun Tzu, The Art of War

The creation of this page was part of a HTML-course as part of my .NET education at IT-högskolan. In a previous course I developed a console application named Debriefer. Its development will continue with more features as my knowledge and skills grow and evolve.

As a wargamer I figured it would be fun and convenient to track Flames of War sessions. Flames of War is a 15mm scale WW2 war game that I recently got into. The idea is to make this tool work with other, similiar, war games.

version 0.1 features

After Action Report

Content

  • Date
  • Resolution
  • Scenario
  • Forces
  • Rounds
  • Players
  • Scores

Player profiles

  • Login function
  • Forces
  • Wins and losses

Forces

  • Name
  • Nation: US, Great Britain, Germany etc
  • Era: early, mid or late war
  • Point cost

Scenarios

  • Free-for-all, Encounter, specific historical battles etc
  • Scenario specific rules, setup and objectives

Rounds

  • Comments
  • Future features will include
    1. Photo upload
    2. Image editor
